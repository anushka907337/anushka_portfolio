An Approach to Subscribing
Nutritional Health with
SUN Minimeal

The MINIMEALS app revolutionizes nutrition by offering a subscription-based service that delivers nutrient-complete meals to your doorstep. With a focus on convenience, these meals are not only rich in essential nutrients from nature, but they also contribute to a sustainable future. As an added incentive, every purchase earns users Sun Scores, which can be redeemed for discounts and rewards, making healthy eating both effortless and rewarding.

Sector
Nutritional, FMCG, Subscription based E-commerce
Challenge
Designing the MINIMEALS app posed several unique challenges. Firstly, crafting an engaging user experience that encourages customers to prioritize better health and set achievable goals for increasing their Sun Scores required thoughtful interface and gamification elements. Secondly, showcasing the product comprehensively across various app sections while maintaining clarity and nutritional detail was a design balancing act. Lastly, presenting detailed nutritional charts in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand manner demanded creative solutions to ensure user comprehension and engagement with the product's benefits.
Project Time
3 weeks (before handoff to development)

Subscription Model

Sun Score Reward System

Purchasing MINIMEALS earns users Sun Scores, which could potentially be used for discounts, rewards, or other incentives, encouraging continued engagement and loyalty.

User-Friendly Interface

Ensure that the app has an intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface. Users should be able to quickly understand how to order these meals.

Informative Content

Include concise, easy-to-understand information about the product's benefits, ingredients, and nutritional value. This will help users make informed decisions.

Ordering Process

Streamline the ordering process to make it as simple as possible. A few taps should be all it takes to order Minimeals.

Feedback Mechanism

Implement a way for users to provide feedback and reviews. Positive user experiences can be a powerful marketing tool.

Retargeting

Retaining MINIMEALS app users through retargeting proved a crucial task. The goal was to re-engage users effectively, emphasizing their health objectives and Sun Score rewards, while avoiding overbearing or repetitive messaging. Striking this balance was pivotal in maintaining user interest and loyalty.
